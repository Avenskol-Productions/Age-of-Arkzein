lever¶

Tags: fort | armok | inspection | productivity | buildings

Command: lever

Inspect and pull levers.

Usage¶

lever list

Print out a list of your fort’s levers, including their name, activation state, and what they are linked to (if anything).

lever pull <id> [<options>]

Queue a job so a dwarf will pull the specified lever. This is the same as q querying the building and queueing a P pull job.

If your levers aren’t named, you can find out a lever’s ID with the bprobe command.

Examples¶

lever pull 42 --priority

Queue a job to pull lever 42 at high priority.

lever pull 42 --now

Skip the job and pull the lever with the hand of Armok!

Options¶

--priority

Queue a job at high priority.

--now

Magically toggle the lever immediately.
